About Us

This Training and Placement Management System has been developed to assist students, faculty, and recruiters in managing campus recruitment activities more efficiently. It serves as a centralized platform to handle student data, training records, job postings, and recruitment schedules. The system is maintained by the institution's Training and Placement Cell, with the goal of ensuring transparency, timely communication, and ease of access to all stakeholders involved in the placement process.
By integrating training updates, placement drives, and communication tools into a single platform, this system aims to simplify the end-to-end placement workflow for both students and recruiters.
